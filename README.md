# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Create visualizations that convey a compelling business story using Tableau.

## Tool Used
Tableau Public

## Dataset
Sample Superstore

## Dashboards
Sales by Product Category,
Profit by Product Sub-Category,
Annual Sales Trend,
Sales by Segment,
Discount vs Profit,
Sales by Region,
Executive Summary

## Key Insights
Technology generated the highest sales.
Copiers generated the highest profit.
Sales showed steady growth after 2015.
Consumer segment contributed the highest revenue.
West region recorded the highest sales.
Higher discounts often reduced profitability.
